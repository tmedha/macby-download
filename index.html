Everything Macby does

A complete clipboard manager, with the privacy bits built in rather than bolted on.

🔍

Searchable history

Every copy is saved and instantly searchable. Text, links, images, and files alike.

✂️

Snip capture

Grab part of your screen with a hotkey. Snips go to your history and a folder you pick.

🔢

OTP auto-clear

Spots one-time passcodes and clears them from the clipboard after you paste, or on a timer.

🔒

Touch ID protection

Detects credit cards and SSNs, and can require Touch ID before pasting them.

🙈

Excluded apps

Copies from your password manager are never recorded. Add any app you like to the list.

⌨️

Global hotkeys

Open history or start a snip from anywhere. Every shortcut is rebindable.

🚀

Launch at login

Lives in the menu bar, starts with your Mac, stays out of the way until you need it.

📄

Paste as plain text

Drop the formatting on paste, and keep items you reuse often at the top of the list.

🛡️

Stays on your Mac

Your history is local. Pause monitoring anytime, or clear the whole thing in one click.

A look inside

Screenshots from the app itself.

Clipboard history

Your whole clipboard, one search away

Hit ⇧⌘V and a popover appears wherever you're working. Type to filter through everything you've copied.

  • Text, links, images, and files side by side
  • Shows which app each item came from
  • Clear the whole history from the bottom

General

Tune it to your workflow

Decide how much history to keep, bubble frequently used items to the top, or pause monitoring when you want privacy. Your snip folder lives here too.

  • Keep last N items, 300 by default
  • Move pasted items to the top of the list
  • Launch at login and pause monitoring

Privacy

Sensitive things stay locked

Macby spots credit card numbers and SSNs, then asks for Touch ID before it lets you paste one. It catches one-time passcodes too, and clears them once you're done.

  • Detects credit cards and SSNs
  • Require Touch ID to paste sensitive items
  • Optional aggressive SSN detection
  • One-time passcodes clear themselves

Shortcuts and snips

Hotkeys your fingers will learn fast

Open history with ⇧⌘V, start a snip with ⇧⌘S, or rebind both to anything you like.

  • Fully customizable global shortcuts
  • Snips land straight in your history
  • Esc cancels while you're recording a key

Excluded apps

Some things shouldn't be remembered

Add your password manager, or any app, to the excluded list. Macby simply won't record what you copy from it.

  • Per app exclusions
  • Built with password managers in mind
  • Those copies are never stored

Never lose a copy again

Download Macby, drag it to Applications, and your clipboard gets a memory.
