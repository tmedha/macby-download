Everything Macby does

Small app, big memory 🧠

A full clipboard manager with thoughtful privacy features baked right in.

🔍

Searchable history

Every copy is saved and instantly searchable — text, links, and images alike. Find that thing from an hour ago in a second.

✂️

Snip capture

Grab any part of your screen with a hotkey. Snips land in your history and save to a folder you choose.

🔢

Smart OTP handling

Spots one-time passcodes and auto-clears them from the clipboard after you paste — or after a timeout. Your call.

🔒

Touch ID protection

Detects credit cards and SSNs, and can require Touch ID before pasting anything sensitive from Macby.

🙈

Excluded apps

Add password managers or any app to the excluded list and Macby will never record what you copy from them.

⌨️

Global hotkeys

Open your history or start a snip from anywhere. Every shortcut is fully rebindable to whatever feels right.

🚀

Launch at login

Quietly lives in your menu bar, starts with your Mac, and stays out of the way until you need it.

📄

Paste as plain text

Strip away fussy formatting on paste, keep frequently-used items at the top, and tune history to your liking.

🛡️

Private by design

Your clipboard history lives on your Mac. Pause monitoring anytime, or clear everything with one click.

🔍 Clipboard history

Your whole clipboard, one search away

Hit the hotkey and a tidy popover appears wherever you're working. Type to filter through everything you've copied.

  • ✓ Text, links, and images side by side
  • ✓ See which app each item came from
  • ✓ Keeps up to your chosen number of items
⇧⌘V to open
⚙️ General

Tune it to your workflow

Decide how much history to keep, bubble frequently-used items to the top, launch at login, or pause monitoring whenever you want privacy.

  • ✓ Keep last N items (300 by default)
  • ✓ Move pasted items to the top of the list
  • ✓ Launch at login & pause monitoring
🔢 One-time passcodes

OTPs that clean up after themselves

Copied text that looks like a 4–8 digit passcode gets flagged and cleared from your clipboard — after you paste, after a timeout, or whichever comes first.

  • ✓ Auto-detects one-time codes
  • ✓ Clear on paste, on timeout, or either
  • ✓ Configurable timeout window
🧼 auto-clear
🔒 Privacy

Sensitive stuff stays locked down

Macby can spot credit card numbers and SSNs, then ask for Touch ID before it lets you paste them. There's even an aggressive SSN mode for the extra-careful.

  • ✓ Detects credit cards & SSNs
  • ✓ Require Touch ID to paste sensitive items
  • ✓ Optional aggressive SSN detection
☝️ Touch ID
⌨️ Shortcuts & snips

Hotkeys your fingers will love

Open your history with ⇧⌘V and start a snip with ⇧⌘S — or rebind them to anything. Snips save to a folder you pick.

  • ✓ Fully customizable global shortcuts
  • ✓ Snips saved to your chosen folder
  • ✓ Also added straight to clipboard history
🙈 Excluded apps

Some things aren't meant to be remembered

Add your password manager — or any app — to the excluded list, and Macby simply won't record what you copy from it. Perfect for secrets.

  • ✓ Per-app exclusions
  • ✓ Great for password managers
  • ✓ Copies from those apps are never stored
